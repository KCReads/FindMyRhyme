Policy

Content & Use Policy This resource is designed to support, not replace, the personal knowledge, creativity, and professional expertise that make storytime meaningful. Tools such as AI may be used to help expand ideas, improve efficiency, and increase access to materials, but all content is thoughtfully reviewed to ensure it offers value in supporting early literacy and engaging young audiences. Some songs or rhymes included here may have been created or supported by AI. Their inclusion reflects a professional determination that they may be useful in a storytime or educational setting. AI-assisted content will be clearly labeled so that users can make informed choices. Because many children’s songs and rhymes come from a long-standing folkloric tradition, some entries may have a problematic or historically harmful background. When included, this is done to promote awareness while also offering alternative options that support the same early literacy goals. Entries with a problematic history will be clearly labeled. This resource is intended to provide options, not prescriptions. Every presenter knows their audience best, and you are encouraged to use your own judgment when selecting materials for your programs. Privacy Favorites and email-sharing features are stored locally on your device. No personal information is collected or stored externally.
