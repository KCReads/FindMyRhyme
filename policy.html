Policy & Disclaimer

Purpose of this database

Find a Rhyme is a discovery tool designed to help users locate songs, rhymes, and related resources for storytime planning and programming.

Use of materials

Users are responsible for reviewing linked content and deciding whether a resource is appropriate for their own setting, audience, and institutional policies.

AI-Supported entries

Some entries may be labeled AI-Supported for transparency. Users can include or exclude those entries based on their own preferences, policies, or professional judgment.

Problematic History

Some entries may be labeled Problematic History to support informed use and thoughtful decision-making. This label is not a directive, but a note for users who want that context.

External links

Find a Rhyme includes links to third-party websites, videos, and supplemental materials. Those outside resources are not controlled by this site and may change, move, or become unavailable over time.

Accuracy and updates

Every effort is made to keep the database accurate and useful, but errors, omissions, and outdated links can happen. Users are encouraged to report issues through the Contact page.

Disclaimer

Inclusion in this database does not constitute endorsement of a creator, platform, viewpoint, or resource. The database is offered as an informational and planning tool.
